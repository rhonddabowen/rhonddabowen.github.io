The Emporium

Download PDF

The Emporium is an urban solution for central Dunedin. It embraces the ideals of a walkable city by providing high quality apartment housing within the inner-city. The ground floor of the new structure is dedicated to an eclectic mix of local food vendors, thereby activating the street front and drawing in foot traffic from neighbouring venues such as the town hall and public library.
